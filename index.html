All About Guinea Pigs

Introduction to Guinea Pigs

Guinea pigs, also known as cavies, are gentle and social animals that make wonderful pets. They are known for their charming personalities and their love of fresh vegetables.

Diet

A healthy guinea pig diet consists of high-quality hay, fresh vegetables, and a limited amount of pellets. It's important to provide a constant supply of fresh water.

Habitat

Guinea pigs need a spacious and clean cage with a soft bedding material. They thrive in a temperature-controlled environment and enjoy having toys and tunnels to explore.

Care

Regular grooming, nail trimming, and health check-ups are essential for a guinea pig's well-being. They are social animals and do best with at least one other guinea pig companion.

Fun Facts

  • Guinea pigs are not from Guinea and are not related to pigs.
  • They are born with their eyes open and a full coat of fur.
  • Guinea pigs "popcorn" when they are happy, which is a series of small jumps and twists.
